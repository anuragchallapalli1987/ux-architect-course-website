Essential Reading

  • Information Architecture (4th Ed)

    Morville, Rosenfeld, and Arango. Master structural patterns, taxonomies, and search findability hierarchies.

  • Designing for the Digital Age

    Kim Goodwin. The ultimate guide to enterprise product scale, research synthesis, and org alignments.

  • Just Enough Research

    Erika Hall. Conduct rapid, strategic, and high-impact mixed-methods research that stakeholders trust.

  • Atomic Design

    Brad Frost. Establish the baseline mental model for reusable component architectures and scalable design systems.
